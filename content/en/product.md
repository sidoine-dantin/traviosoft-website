## Requests & client CRM

**Benefit:** Never lose a client request again, and keep the full history in one place.

Key points:
- All incoming requests centralized with a clear status (new, awaiting reply, handled)
- AI-assisted reply drafts to save time on writing (always reviewed and sent by you)
- Full client profile: contact details, notes, tax information adapted to your country
- Complete history per client: requests, quotes, orders, invoices

**What it changes:** No more digging through email to find out where a client stands: it's all in their profile.

---

## Quotes

**Benefit:** Professional quotes in minutes, with your margins calculated automatically.

Key points:
- Build quotes line by line (catalog items or free-text lines)
- Total and margin recalculated automatically with every change
- PDF export ready to send to the client
- Quote statuses (draft, sent, accepted, refused) and an expiration date
- Payment milestones defined directly on the quote

**What it changes:** A quote that used to take two hours in Excel now takes minutes, with a clean PDF output.

---

## Orders & operations

**Benefit:** As soon as a quote is accepted, the preparation work starts automatically.

Key points:
- The order is created automatically from the accepted quote
- Preparation tasks are generated automatically from the quote's line items
- PDF service sheets for the day of the service
- Order status tracking (awaiting payment, paid, in progress, completed)
- A payment link (WooCommerce) can be attached to the order

**What it changes:** Nobody has to wonder "what's left to prepare for this file?": the task list already exists.

---

## Suppliers & guides

**Benefit:** All the information about your suppliers and guides, in one place, kept up to date.

Key points:
- Supplier and guide profiles: contacts, banking details, tax information
- Associated documents (contracts, certificates, etc.)
- Languages spoken, categories and geographic areas
- Multiple contacts per supplier

**What it changes:** No more digging through email to find a guide's phone number or the latest version of a contract.

---

## Catalog

**Benefit:** Your services, organized and ready to drop into a quote.

Key points:
- Catalog of products and services with categories
- Seasonal pricing (different prices depending on the period)
- Direct link between a catalog item and a supplier
- Purchase price and sales price tracked separately, to keep visibility on margins

**What it changes:** Building a quote becomes a matter of picking from an already up-to-date list, instead of re-entering prices every time.

---

## Projects

**Benefit:** For complex files, keep multiple alternatives and the traveler list organized.

Key points:
- Full lifecycle of a travel project
- Multiple alternatives (variants) for the same project
- Named list of travelers per project

**What it changes:** Ideal when a client is choosing between several versions of a trip, or for group files with a long list of participants.

---

## Payments & invoicing

**Benefit:** See at a glance who has paid, who still owes, and where you stand with your suppliers.

Key points:
- Payment milestones per quote, with due dates
- Tracking of payment reminders
- Client invoices with detailed line items and statuses (draft, issued, partially paid, paid, overpaid, cancelled)
- Supplier invoices tracked at the same level of detail

**What it changes:** No more parallel spreadsheets to track payments: everything is linked to the original quote and order.

---

## Reports

**Benefit:** A bird's-eye view of your business to step back and see the bigger picture.

Key points:
- An overview dashboard of activity (quotes, orders, payments)
- Always up to date, built on the same data as the rest of the tool

**What it changes:** No need to build a separate dashboard: the numbers are already there.

---

## AI assistant

**Benefit:** An assistant that saves you time, without ever acting on your behalf without approval.

Key points:
- Helps draft replies to client requests (a draft is proposed, never sent automatically)
- Built-in help chatbot to answer questions about how to use the tool
- Operational assistant that can read your data and propose actions (create a client, update a request, etc.)

**"Zero automatic writes" callout:** No assistant suggestion is applied to your data without your explicit approval. You stay in control at every step.

**What it changes:** AI does the first draft, you make the final call.

---

## Multi-language

**Benefit:** An interface built for your team and your international clients.

Key points:
- Interface available in French, English and Spanish
- Tax information handling adapted by country
