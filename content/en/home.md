## The reality check

**Section title:** Does this sound familiar?

Three pain points, presented as cards:

1. **Quotes that take hours**
   Every quote is rebuilt from scratch in Excel or Word, margins are recalculated by hand, and the final result doesn't always look as professional as you'd like when sending it to a client.

2. **Payment tracking by gut feeling**
   Who paid the deposit? Who still owes the balance before departure? Without a dedicated tool, this lives in a spreadsheet, or in your head, with the risk of forgetting a reminder.

3. **Supplier coordination over email and WhatsApp**
   Guides, hotels, transport providers: every important piece of information (contact details, documents, languages spoken, rates) is scattered across chat threads and attachments.

## The solution

## The 4 pillars

**Section title:** What Traviosoft changes in practice

### 1. Save time on repetitive tasks
Build your quotes line by line, with automatic total and margin calculation. Export to PDF in one click. As soon as a quote is accepted, the order and its tasks are created automatically.

### 2. Make the client relationship more professional
Quotes and service sheets in clean PDF format, structured tracking of every request (new, awaiting reply, handled), and a full history per client.

### 3. Stay on top of payments and invoicing
Set payment milestones directly on the quote, attach a payment link to each one, and track client and supplier invoices with clear statuses (draft, issued, partially paid, paid).

### 4. Centralize suppliers, guides and your catalog
Supplier and guide profiles with contacts, documents and languages spoken. A service catalog with categories and seasonal pricing. Everything connected to your quotes and orders.
