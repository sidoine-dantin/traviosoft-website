## Intro

**Headline:** Frequently asked questions

**Text:** If your question isn't here, ask it directly during the demo.

---

## Is my data secure?

Your data is hosted securely and only accessible to authorized users at your agency. Access to AI features is under your control: the AI can read certain data to assist you, but it never writes to your database without your explicit approval.

---

## How long does it take to get started?

Setup time depends on your current situation (number of clients, ongoing quotes, suppliers to register). During the demo, we'll assess together how much time it would take to get started with your real data.

---

## Is it suitable if I work alone? What about a team of 10 or 15?

Yes, in both cases. Traviosoft is designed for teams of 2 to 15 people, but it can also be used effectively by a single person handling the whole process, from quote to invoice.

---

## Can we import our existing clients, quotes or suppliers?

Depending on the format of your current data (Excel files, exports from another tool), an import is possible. This is a point to discuss during the demo to figure out the best approach for your situation.

---

## Does the AI access my data without my consent?

No. AI access to your data is an option you control. Even when enabled, the AI can read certain information to assist you (for example, to suggest a reply draft), but it never modifies your data without your explicit approval.

---

## Is the software available in my language?

Traviosoft's interface is available in French, English and Spanish.

---

## What happens if I want to cancel?

You remain the owner of your data. If you cancel, a data export can be arranged. The exact terms are detailed in our terms of service and can be discussed with you directly.

---

## Does Traviosoft replace my website or my accounting software?

No. Traviosoft is an internal management tool for running your quotes, orders, payments and suppliers. It doesn't replace your website (showcase, public online booking) or your accounting software, although it can make your accountant's job easier thanks to the tracking of client and supplier invoices.
