# Product

## Register

brand

## Users

Boutique travel agency owners and directors - typically the same person. Teams of 2 to 15 people running tailor-made trips (luxury, DMC, specialist receptives). Priority market: Spain (Barcelona, Madrid, Seville, Valencia). Also relevant for French-speaking and English-speaking boutique agencies. They are not sysadmins - they know their business deeply, not software. They are currently running their operations on Excel, Word, email and WhatsApp.

## Product Purpose

Traviosoft is a management tool for boutique travel agencies. It covers the full client file lifecycle: client request, quote (PDF), order, supplier coordination, tracked payments, invoicing. The website's only conversion goal is to get visitors to book a 30-minute demo. No self-serve signup, no pricing table - just one call to action repeated across all pages.

## Brand Personality

Confident, clean, human. Direct and concrete - no marketing fluff, no SaaS buzzwords like "revolutionary" or "disruptive". Empathetic toward the daily reality of running a boutique agency. Transparent about being a young product built from real-world use rather than claiming a large client base. Tone: like a knowledgeable peer, not a salesperson.

## Anti-references

- Generic SaaS landing pages with hero-metric grids (big numbers, gradient accents)
- Sites that claim "100+ happy clients" or show fake testimonials
- Overly polished enterprise software marketing (Salesforce, HubSpot aesthetic)
- Any design that feels disconnected from the travel and hospitality world
- Glassmorphism, gradient text, side-stripe borders

## Design Principles

1. **Substance over decoration** - every section earns its place by answering a real question the user has while reading the page
2. **Honest by default** - never imply scale, testimonials or features that don't exist; the product's credibility comes from specificity, not from inflated claims
3. **One conversion path** - every page ends at the same place: book a demo; no competing CTAs, no subscriptions, no pricing games
4. **Built for the expert** - assume the reader knows their business better than we do; address pain points precisely rather than explaining what a boutique agency is
5. **Multilingual by nature** - FR/EN/ES are equal citizens, not afterthoughts; the language switcher is visible on every page

## Accessibility & Inclusion

WCAG 2.1 AA minimum. Reduced motion support required (agencies are often running on older hardware or in high-contrast environments). All interactive elements keyboard-accessible. Language switcher must be clearly labeled and functional without JavaScript for screen readers.
